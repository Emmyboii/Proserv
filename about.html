Who We Are

Proserv Emergency Services Inc is a trusted name in fast, professional emergency response solutions across Chicago and surrounding areas. Headquartered at 6136 N Winchester Avenue STE 2H, Chicago, Illinois, we provide critical support to residential and commercial clients when they need it most.

Whether it’s fixing a faulty outlet, repairing a leaky faucet, installing shelving, or troubleshooting minor wiring issues, our skilled technicians handle each task with care, efficiency, and attention to detail. No job is too small — and every job is important to us.

At Proserv, we understand that emergencies don’t wait — and neither do we. Our mission is to help you recover quickly, safely, and with minimal disruption. From the moment you call us, we prioritize clear communication, rapid mobilization, and meticulous care for your property and well-being.
